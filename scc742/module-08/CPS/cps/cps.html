Hexelo Certificate Practice Statement

This is a placeholder document for the Certificate Practice Statement. This document describes the operational procedures at the CA level. It includes, but is not limited to, the following information:

  • Certificate Authority identifying information
  • Certificate policies and certificates types that are issued by the CA
  • Policies and procedures for issuing and revoking certificates
  • Polices and procedures for publishing Certificate Revocation List (CRL) and publication intervals
  • Lifetimes for issued certificate types
  • Cryptopgraphic algorithms, key lengths, and crytographic service providers (CSPs)
  • Certificate renewal policies and procedures, including renewal of CA certificate
