Hexelo Medium Assurance Signing Policy

Signing certificates are used to verify the identity of the subject of the certificate.

Medium assurance signing certificates are issued only after successful authentication in a face-to-face meeting using a valid government-issued photo ID, such as a driver's license, passport, or Nexus card. The Local Registration Authority (LRA) must record the details of the government ID and store these details securely before issuing the certificate.
